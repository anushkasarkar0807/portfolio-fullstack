Integrated Sensing & Communication (ISAC) (localization)

ISAC combines wireless data communication and environmental sensing in a unified framework, leveraging advanced AI/ML to enable spectrum sharing and multi-functionality for next-generation 5G/6G networks.

Overview

  • Domain: Wireless Communication, Signal Processing
  • Core Concepts: Spectrum Sharing, Joint Sensing & Communication
  • Technologies: AI/ML, 5G/6G, MIMO, Radar

Features

Features

  • Joint waveform design for data & sensing.
  • Real-time spatial mapping & localization.
  • AI-based activity and object detection.
  • Optimized resource sharing for 6G platforms.

Research Outcomes

  • Enhanced spectrum efficiency for communication networks.
  • Improved low-latency, high-accuracy object/person localization.
  • Demonstrated interference mitigation in ISAC simulations.
